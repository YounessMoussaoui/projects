We were partners with the Center for Access to Bio-innovation Technology at Collège la Cité to respond to the growing need for innovation in the inventory management of cannabis seedlings for
Cannabis farmers in Canada.


The goal of this project is to demonstrate how our software solution is designed to address the unique challenges of the cannabis industry which became legal in canada.
